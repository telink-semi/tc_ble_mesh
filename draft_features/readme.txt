How to provide sig mesh SDK v3.2.0 and documents to customer?
1.	Find the latest SDK from gitlab http://192.168.48.36/sdk_app/ble_mesh/telink_sig_mesh_sdk/-/releases, and download it
2.	Ask customer to register an account on https://www.bluetooth.com/ 
3.	Share the sig mesh SDK and documents to customer 

In future, The SDK released on wiki will not contain the features of Bluetooth SIG draft specification, if customer interested in the features, FAE team can  follow the above steps, verify the qualification of customer, and then share the files to customer.
